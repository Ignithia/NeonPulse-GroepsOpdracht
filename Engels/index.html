Who are we?

At Global Foodies, people from all walks of life come together. Regardless of your age, background, or experience, everyone is welcome! At Global Foodies, it’s not just about cooking – members connect with each other by trying dishes from different cultures and sharing their stories. Global Foodies is committed to avoiding food waste and making a positive contribution to our planet. You’re welcome too! Become a member of Global Foodies and discover new dishes today!
