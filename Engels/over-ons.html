About us

Connection through cooking

At Global Foodies, it's all about connection through cooking. We explore new flavors and techniques together, with respect for traditions and a focus on sustainability. By using local products and being creative with ingredients, we make cooking both fun and mindful. Everyone is welcome, from beginners to experienced chefs, to learn and enjoy together.

Our mission

We want to provide a platform where cooking enthusiasts can share their passion and grow together.

Our vision

We aim to inspire people to discover new flavors and cultures by using local products and reducing food waste.

How do we distinguish ourselves?

We distinguish ourselves by making our activities as affordable as possible for everyone. This means we don’t want our members to pay out of their own pockets; instead, we finance our workshops through sales campaigns. It’s also a place to make friends, where social interaction is key. We encourage this through fun games, team-building activities, and more. We are a creative cooking club, meaning we don’t shy away from challenges and always strive to make the best of every situation. Everyone is welcome, from beginners to professionals.
